Trust and Safety

Your safety and trust are our top priorities at JoB BoARD.

Our Commitment

At JoB BoARD, we are committed to providing a safe and trustworthy environment for job seekers and employers. Our platform includes various measures to ensure the safety and security of our users.

Verification Process

We verify all job postings and employer profiles to ensure legitimacy. Employers must provide valid information, which we review before approving their postings.

Privacy Protection

We take your privacy seriously. Personal information shared on our platform is protected through robust security measures and is never disclosed without your consent.

Reporting and Support

If you encounter any suspicious activity or have concerns about a job posting, please report it immediately. Our support team is available 24/7 to address any issues and provide assistance.

Community Guidelines

We expect all users to adhere to our community guidelines, which promote respectful and professional interactions. Violations of these guidelines will result in appropriate actions, including removal from the platform.
