Welcome to JoB BoARD

A platform founded in 2024 by a group of passionate students to help the people of Cameroon find jobs.

About Us

JoB BoARD was born out of a desire to address the unemployment crisis in Cameroon. The founding members, a team of recent graduates and students from various universities across the country, shared a common vision of creating a platform that would empower individuals to secure meaningful employment opportunities.

After witnessing the struggles faced by friends and family members in their job search, the team decided to take action. With their combined skills in programming, design, and business, they embarked on a journey to build JoB BoARD from the ground up.

Months of hard work and dedication culminated in the launch of JoB BoARD in early 2024. From the outset, the platform garnered attention for its user-friendly interface, comprehensive job listings, and innovative approach to connecting job seekers with employers.

One of the key principles that guided the development of JoB BoARD was accessibility. Recognizing the socioeconomic disparities present in Cameroon, the founders made the decision to offer the platform free of charge for the first year of use. This decision was motivated by their belief that everyone deserves equal access to opportunities, regardless of their financial circumstances.

Looking ahead, the team at JoB BoARD remains committed to their mission of transforming the job market in Cameroon. They are constantly exploring new ways to improve the platform, expand its reach, and support both job seekers and employers on their journey towards success.
