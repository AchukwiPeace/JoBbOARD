Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form. You'll need to provide your name, email address, and create a password.

How do I post a job?

Once you have created an account, navigate to the "Post a Job" section, fill out the job details, and click "Submit". Your job posting will be reviewed and published shortly.

How can I apply for a job?

To apply for a job, simply browse the job listings, select a job you're interested in, and click the "Apply" button. You'll need to upload your resume and a cover letter.

Is there a fee to use JoB BoARD?

JoB BoARD is free for the first year of use. After that, there will be subscription plans available for premium features and services.
